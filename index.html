Wine is many things to many people. It’s a passion, career, hobby, beverage, meal accompaniment, investment or simply a fun way to take away from the drudgeries of everyday life. For others it’s a place holder, allowing them to remember events and there are those to simply enjoy its hedonistic pleasures. Regardless of how you look at wine, there is always something new for everyone to learn about wine.

Wine Tourism

Wine tourism, enotourism or vinitourism is a relatively new form of tourism which consists in visits to wineries, tasting wines, vineyard walks, or even taking an active part in the harvest.
